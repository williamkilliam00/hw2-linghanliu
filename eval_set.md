# Evaluation Set: Meeting Summarizer
**Workflow:** Extracting Action Items from meeting notes.

## Test Cases
1. **Normal:** "Sarah will draft social media posts by Tuesday. Mike will contact influencers."
2. **Edge Case:** "The team had pizza and talked about the weather. No work was discussed."
3. **Fail Case:** "Alice will do the report. No, wait, Bob will. Actually, Alice should do it but not send it."
